Introduction

What is a webmaster and role does the webmaster play?

A webmaster is responsible for the public image of the club as it relates to Internet-based mediums like a website or social media. He or she creates, updates, maintains a club website appropriately, and manages the social media accounts for the club

Roles of a Webmaster

  • Create a Website
  • Manage the created Website
  • Disperse reliable information through Social Media
  • Engage with Key Clubbers through the Internet
  • Help alleviate technical issues in the club
