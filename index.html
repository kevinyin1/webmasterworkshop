Introduction

What is a Webmaster?

A webmaster is responsible for the public image of the club as it relates to Internet-based mediums like a website or social media. He or she creates, updates, and maintains a club website where appropriate, and he or she manages the social media accounts for the club

Roles of a Webmaster

  • Create a Website
  • Manage the created Website
  • Disperse reliable information through Social Media
  • Engage with Key Clubbers through the Internet
  • Help alleviate technology issues within the home club
